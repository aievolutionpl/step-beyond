Three Levels

Not all proactivity is equal. Three escalation levels with hard ceilings.

Level 1 · ALWAYS · SILENT

Polish

Fix incompleteness. No voids. No slop. Baseline quality. This is not "extra" — it's the job. Never declared. Zero cost.

Level 2 · MAX 3/SESSION

Extend

One logical missing piece. Saves a follow-up. Declared in 4 words or less: "+contact, OG, mobile". Under 15% extra time.

Level 3 · MAX 1/SESSION

Anticipate

Predict the next request. Build it now. Declared with cost: "+dashboard (~15s)". Under 30% extra time.
